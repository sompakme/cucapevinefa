    Where can I spend Spirit Orbs?

    Once you have four Spirit Orbs, you can visit a statue of the Goddess Hylia to turn the Spirit Orbs into Heart Containers or more Stamina bars. Poes can be exchanged at any Bargainer Statue you find in Tears of the Kingdom.

    10 Aug 2024, 00:00

    1. How do I turn Spirit Orbs into Heart Containers or Stamina bars?

    Once you have four Spirit Orbs, you can visit a statue of the Goddess Hylia to turn the Spirit Orbs into Heart Containers or more Stamina bars.

    2. Where do I trade Spirit Orbs in Tears of the Kingdom?

    Poes can be exchanged at any Bargainer Statue you find in Tears of the Kingdom.

    3. What can I do with soul orbs?

    Collecting Soul Orbs and powerups with Clash Mode enabled will grant protector skulls from [4-S: CLASH OF THE BRANDICOOT]; normal blue Soul Orbs will grant the equivalent of one protector, red Soul Orbs grant two, and both Dual Wield Orbs and normal Dual Wield powerups grant three.

    4. How many Spirit Orbs are in Tears of the Kingdom?

    There are 7 in total. For each orb you bring back, Rotana will give you a Hydromelon.

    5. Where can you pray in Breath of the Wild?

    You can pray at the following Goddess Statues: Temple of Time (Great Plateau), Hateno Village, Kakariko Village, Tarrey Town (following completion of the From the Ground Up Side Quest), Kara Kara Bazaar, Gerudo Town, Rito Village, and Goron City.

    6. How do you pray in Zelda?

    At any Goddess Statue, you can pray to trade in the Light of Blessings you receive from completing shrines. Trading in four Light of Blessings will let you gain either an additional Heart Container or an additional Stamina Vessel.

    7. Can you get the Master Sword with 12 hearts?

    No, to obtain the Master Sword you will need 13 full heart containers.

    8. Will the Triforce be in Tears of the Kingdom?

    The Triforce does not appear when Zelda uses magic in Tears of the Kingdom.

    9. How long do soul orbs last?

    Soul Orbs last for 3 seconds once an enemy is killed by Reyna.

    10. What are eternal orbs used for?

    Eternal Orbs are an in-game currency used in Diablo Immortal to purchase gameplay-enhancing Eternal Legendary Crests, cosmetics, and more.

    11. Can you get all 40 hearts in Tears of the Kingdom?

    No, as of now the maximum amount of both resources Link can hold is 40 Hearts or 38 Hearts and 3 full Stamina Wheels.

    12. Can you max both hearts and stamina in Tears of the Kingdom?

    No, you can only max out one stat – either hearts or stamina.

    13. How many shrines are in Tears of the Kingdom?

    There are 152 Shrines in total in Tears of the Kingdom, 120 on the surface and 32 in the sky.

    14. Is the Hylian Shield unbreakable?

    No, the Hylian Shield can break. It has a vast amount of durability but is not invincible.

    15. Why is Master Sword only 30?

    The Master Sword deals only 30 damage and wears out quickly when attacking regular enemies because it had been broken and worn down a hundred years ago in the battles during the Great Calamity.

    16. Which is the hardest divine beast?

    Naboris has been known to be the hardest of all the Divine Beasts.

    17. Will there be DLC for Tears of the Kingdom?

    No, there will not be any DLC for Tears of the Kingdom.

    18. How old is Link in Tears of the Kingdom?

    Link is around 122-125 years old in Tears of the Kingdom.

    19. Will Tears of the Kingdom end Zelda?

    No, Tears of the Kingdom will not be the end of the Zelda franchise.

    20. How do I activate Urbosa?

    Trigger Urbosa’s Fury with a charge attack by holding Y.

    21. Is there a Goddess Statue in Hateno?

    Yes, there is a Goddess statue in Hateno Village. It is located just past the Dye Shop heading into the Village on the left-hand side.

    22. Is there a Goddess Statue in Korok Forest?

    Yes, there is a Goddess statue in Korok Forest. It is located to the left of the Spore Store.
