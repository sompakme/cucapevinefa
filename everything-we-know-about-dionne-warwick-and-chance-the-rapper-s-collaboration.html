    Dionne Warwick and Chance The Rapper are working together for the greater good. Their collaboration, "Nothing's Impossible," is a charity duet to raise money for the two artists' chosen charities. Warwick announced an update on the collab on Twitter in January 2021. "Chance and I will be getting in the studio very, very soon," the "Say A Little Prayer" singer said. "It's gonna be a pleasure working with him and his organization, called SocialWorks, as he's working with mine, Hunger: Not Impossible."

    She continued, "We're going to try to do some wonderful things for people that are desperately in need... so keep your eyes and ears open, because we're getting in that studio soon and we're going to give you something that you cannot resist." Chance shared the video and added, "THIS IS GOING TO BE SO GOOD FOR SO MANY!! THANK YOU DIONNE THE SINGER!! WE GON MAKE A DIFFERENCE WITH THIS ONE!!" 

    Warwick previously mentioned that The Weeknd would be joining the pair for the collaboration, per NME. However, it has yet to be confirmed if the "Save Your Tears" singer will be featured on the charity track as of this report. Regardless, we cannot wait to hear the beautiful music these greats are bound to create!
