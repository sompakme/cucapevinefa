    It's no secret that actress, producer, and LolaVie founder Jennifer Aniston has always been a trendsetter. From the classic '90s haircut worn by her Friends character Rachel Green to her ongoing partnership with Vital Proteins, it's clear the star is well-versed in all things beauty and wellness—especially when it comes to maintaining glowy skin.

    In a recent Instagram Stories post, The Morning Show star revealed she's a fan of Tatcha's best-selling The Serum Stick, which uses a combination of squalane, Japanese lemon, and other key ingredients to minimize dryness and signs of aging. Her makeup artist, Angela Levin, uses the handy product to prep Aniston's under-eye and laugh line areas ahead of on-screen makeup. Since the featherweight stick is designed to seamlessly glide on top of skin while locking in its moisture, it's ideal for brightening dark circles and smoothing fine lines.

    Skincare enthusiasts on Amazon are also raving about the top-rated product. "Very pleased with this gem of a product," writes one satisfied shopper of Tatcha's The Serum Stick. "It is so soft and silky on my skin around my eyes—no irritation at all!" Another Amazon customer writes, "I love this for travel. Since we are limited on how much liquid on a plane, anything I can get in powder or solid form such as this stick serum is perfect."
