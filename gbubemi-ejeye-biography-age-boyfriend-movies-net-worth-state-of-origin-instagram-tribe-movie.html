    Biography

    Oritsegbubemi Ejeye (born June 29, 1995) is a creative Nollywood actress and model. She is professionally known as Gbubemi Ejeye. 

    A Law graduate from Houdegbe North American University, she has appeared in many successful short films, web series, drama series, TV series, and feature-length films. 

    In 2022, Gbubemi Ejeye starred in the Netflix Original Drama Series Far From Home as Adufe.

    Early Life

    Gbubemi Ejeye was born into the household of Mr. and Mrs. Ejeye. She was birthed in a private-owned hospital in Lagos City on the 29th of June 1995. 

    She spent her formative years residing in a middle-class neighbourhood in Lagos State. Although she is Lagos-bred, Gbubemi Ejeye is originally from the South-South region. She is an Itsekiri woman who is from Delta State. 

    As a young girl, Gbubemi Ejeye aspired to be a lawyer and worked hard at school to achieve this goal. As she grew older, she discovered she had developed a love for acting. This interest in acting came from participating in school plays and musicals. Over time, her love of acting displaced her desire to practice law. 

    Education

    Gbubemi Ejeye is from a middle-class family and was opportune to receive quality formal education.

    She attended leading private schools in Lagos for her elementary and secondary education. She graduated from high school in 2010 with good grades in her SSCE. 

    Afterwards, Gbubemi Ejeye was admitted to Houdegbe North American University, located in the Republic of Benin. She bagged a Bachelor’s Degree In Law from the institution in 2015.

    Career

    After completing her undergrad studies, Gbubemi Ejeye returned to Nigeria to observe the mandatory National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) program. Upon completing the program, she began working in the media industry. 

    Her acting career began in November 2018 when she performed in a stage play Color Me Pink at Terra Kulture. The stage play was about breast cancer. Her success in stage performance paved the way for her to join the Nollywood industry. 

    Gbubemi Ejeye began auditioning for film roles in Lagos and was cast to play characters in some of these films. Over the years, she has cut her teeth in feature-length films, short films, web series, drama series, and TV series. Notable mentions include Rickety, Oga Pastor, Disruption, Jumbled, The Waitress, When Ex’s Meet, Daughters, The Catalyst, Who’s The Perp, Come To My House, What Women Want, The Matron, and Physical Evidence, amongst others. 

    In 2022, Gbubemi Ejeye played the role of Eniola in the Second Season of the Ndani TV Web Series Game On. In September 2022, she joined the cast of AM Covenant and played the character Talia in the series. She attained international fame for her role in the Netflix Original Drama series Far From Home. She portrays the character Adufe in the series. 

    Gbubemi Ejeye acted alongside Nollywood stars such as Mike Afolarin, Tomi Ojo, Raymond Umenze, Genoveva Umeh, Ruby Okezie (Ruby Precious Okezie), Emeka Nwagbaraocha, Olumide Oworu, and Natse Jemide. 

    In 2023, it was announced that Gbubemi Ejeye had made it to the cast of MTV Shuga Naija Season 5, set to be released later in the year.

    Personal Life

    Gbubemi Ejeye is currently 27 years old. The gorgeous actress is one of the fast-rising female stars in the Nollywood industry. She is dark-skinned and 5.5 feet tall. 

    Besides working as an actress, she is also a thriving commercial model. She has worked with various creative and art directors on several modelling gigs over the years. She was on the cover art and music video of Tiwa Savage‘s hit single 49-99 released in 2019.

    Although there have been some speculations that Gbubemi Ejeye is in a romantic relationship with fellow Far From Home actor Mike Afolarin, this is untrue. The duo are only friends and not lovebirds. However, her relationship status is unknown because she has been private about her personal life. 

    The talented actress resides in a serviced apartment in Lekki, Lagos. 

    Net Worth

    Gbubemi Ejeye makes a good income from her acting and modelling careers. She is currently estimated to be worth $100,000 to $300,000 (USD).
