    Early Life and Inheritance

    Lisa Marie Presley was born into fame and fortune as the only child of Elvis and Priscilla Presley. Upon her father’s death in 1977, she became the heir to the Presley estate. However, she did not gain full control over her inheritance until her 25th birthday. This inheritance included Graceland, the iconic home of Elvis Presley, and a trust fund that was estimated to be worth over $100 million at the time she inherited it.

    Music Career Contributions to Net Worth

She released her debut album “To Whom It May Concern” in 2003, which was certified gold and contributed to her net worth. Subsequent albums, including “Now What” and “Storm & Grace,” added to her earnings, although they did not achieve the same level of commercial success as her first.
