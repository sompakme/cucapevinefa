    Top Nigerian singers, Olamide and Bella Shmurda have gotten many fans talking after a throwback video of them emerged online.

    In a video posted on Instagram by @tooxclusive and spotted by Legit.ng, a young Bella Shmurda was seen crying hard after Olamide helped his career upon meeting him for the first time.

    It was gathered that the YBNL boss had jumped on the remix of Bella Shmurda’s Vision 2020 song.
