Can brown palm tree leaves turn green again?

24 Aug 2024, 00:00

Completely brown fronds are dead, they will not turn green again. Palm trees shed dead fronds as new fronds grow. Green fronds will remain on the majority of the canopy throughout this natural shedding process. How do you revive a brown palm tree? Water and fertilize the tree if this is dry. If it feels soft, you make want to cut back on watering. If the lower fronds are turning brown, this is probably no cause for alarm.
