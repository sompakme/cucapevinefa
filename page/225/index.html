Olive Uniacke Leaving Lionsgate for WME

17 Jul 2024, 00:00

Lionsgate executive Olive Uniacke is now an agent at WME, The Hollywood Reporter has learned. She joined Lionsgate as a vice president of production and development in April 2017. Uniacke, who will be based in WME’s Beverly Hills office, also previously worked for U.K. management-production company 42, for whom she co-produced Anthony Byrne’s 2018 thriller In Darkness. She got her start in film working as an assistant on the U.K. sets of such productions as Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, X-Men: First Class, Fast & Furious 6 and Muppets Most Wanted.
