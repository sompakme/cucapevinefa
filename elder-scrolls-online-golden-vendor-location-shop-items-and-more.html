    First Published May 22, 2022, 12:18

    Elder Scrolls Online (ESO) has a variety of merchant NPCs across Tamriel, selling a broad range of items with various types, rarities, and usages. Some merchants don’t sell items and can instead provide beneficial services to players in exchange for Gold.

    Adhazabi Aba-daro, the Golden Vendor, is one of the many merchants you can find in Tamriel, with her location being based on which alliance you choose when starting the game. This guide will explain how you can find her, the types of items she stocks, and what’s available in Elder Scrolls Online.

    Elder Scrolls Online Golden Vendor – Location

    The Golden Vendor, Adhazabi Aba-daro, is a limited-time merchant that appears in Cyrodiil. Depending on your chosen alliance, she can be found near the Northern High Rock Gate (Daggerfall Covenant), Western Elsweyr Gate (Aldmeri Dominion), or Southern Morrowind Gate (Ebonheart Pact).

    Adhazabi, or Golden as she’s known to the ESO community, only appears during weekends when she sells rare items that would otherwise be available through completing specific quests, expansions, and downloadable content. She will appear at any of the locations mentioned above in Cyrodiil every Friday at 12 AM GMT before closing her shop on Monday at 12 PM GMT.

    Elder Scrolls Online Golden Vendor – Shop Inventory

    Adhazabi’s shop refreshes every week before appearing in-game; however, the types of items she sells remain the same. She’ll have four various sets of jewelry. Two sets are sourced from dungeons, and the remaining two sets are Overland sets, which are unique to a particular zone or area and are often rare enemy drops.

    Additionally, she sells high-rarity monster set pieces acquired from dungeons. Therefore, players need to seek her out every weekend as the items offered aren’t repeated frequently, so ensure that you have enough Alliance Points or Gold before visiting her.

    Elder Scrolls Online Golden Vendor – Week 20 Shop Items

    Adhazabi sells various rare items every weekend. As mentioned earlier, her shop's inventory will refresh to have new offerings available for purchase weekly. Below, we’ve outlined the latest items available in Adhazabi’s shop for you to acquire in Week 20:

    • Arkasis’ Genius: Necklace piece for 250,000 Gold or 500,000 Alliance Points
    • Domihaus: Hat piece for 1000,000 Gold or 200,000 Alliance Points
    • Domihaus: Helm piece for 100,000 Gold or 200,000 Alliance Points
    • Hatchling’s Shell: Necklace piece for 150,000 Gold or 300,000 Alliance Points
    • Hollowfang Thirst: Necklace piece for 250,000 Gold or 500,000 Alliance Points
    • Lady Thorn: Hat piece for 100,000 Gold or 200,000 Alliance Points
    • Lady Thorn: Helm piece for 100,000 Gold or 200,000 Alliance Points
    • Senchal Defender: Necklace piece for 150,000 Gold or 300,000 Alliance Points

    These shop items are available for Week 20, which expires on Monday, 23rd May 2022, at 12 PM GMT in Elder Scrolls Online. Adhazabi will return to the exact locations for Week 21 with new items available.
