    Big Reward Offered For Return Of Sheamus Stolen Celtic Cross Necklace

    WWEs ThunderDome was robbed and Sheamus OF Celtic Cross was lifted. Were not sure what else was stolen, but police are still looking for who pulled off the heist. They have also offered quite a reward for the return of that stolen piece of WWE history.

    17 Sep 2024, 00:00

    WWE’s ThunderDome was robbed and Sheamus’ OF Celtic Cross was lifted. We’re not sure what else was stolen, but police are still looking for who pulled off the heist. They have also offered quite a reward for the return of that stolen piece of WWE history.

    Click here for photos of the man who robbed WWE ThunderDome while they were in the Yuengling Center.

    The Tampa Crime Stoppers put a new bounty out for Sheamus’ Celtic Cross necklace. They are putting up $5,000 for the return of this artifact. The Denver Post reports that they are in search for signed WWE Championships and turnbuckle pads as well.

    Crime Stoppers of Tampa Bay is also offering a $5,000 reward for information about the theft in May of the necklace, three title belts signed by various WWE stars and a corner pad.

    Hopefully, someone will see this money and realize that it’s a much better idea to return the item than try to hock it on the black market. It is a shame that this happened in the first place. We can only hope that all of the items are returned.
