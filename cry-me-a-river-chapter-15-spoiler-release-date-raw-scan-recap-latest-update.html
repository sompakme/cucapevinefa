    Cry Me A River Chapter 15: If you are a fan of the romantic comedy webtoon Cry Me A River, you might be wondering when the next chapter of the hilarious story will be released. The webtoon, written and illustrated by Lee Hyun, follows the love-hate relationship between Lee Ji-hoon, a popular singer, and Kim Ha-na, a reporter who hates his guts. The webtoon is full of funny moments, witty dialogues, and heartwarming scenes that will make you laugh and cry.

    The webtoon is currently in its second season, which started on September 23, 2023. The latest chapter of the webtoon, chapter 13, was released on October 28, 2023. In this chapter, we see Lee Ji-hoon and Kim Ha-na go on a fake date to fool the paparazzi and the fans. However, things got complicated when they ran into Lee Ji-hoon’s ex-girlfriend, Park Soo-yeon, who still had feelings for him. She tried to sabotage their date and make Kim Ha-na jealous, but her plan backfired when Lee Ji-hoon defended Kim Ha-na and kissed her in front of everyone.

    The chapter ended with a cliffhanger, as Lee Ji-hoon and Kim Ha-na realized that their kiss was caught on camera and went viral on the internet. They were shocked and embarrassed by the unexpected turn of events and wondered how they would deal with the consequences.
