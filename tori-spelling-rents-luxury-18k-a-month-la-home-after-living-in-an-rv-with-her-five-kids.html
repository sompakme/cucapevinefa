    Tori Spelling rents luxury $18k-a-month LA home after living in an RV with her five kids

    From the glamorous locales of Beverly Hills, 90210 to the challenging personal twists and turns of recent years, Tori Spelling's life has always captivated public interest. Now, as she seeks a new beginning, the accomplished actress has made a lavish decision for her and her family.

    01 Oct 2024, 00:00

    From the glamorous locales of Beverly Hills, 90210 to the challenging personal twists and turns of recent years, Tori Spelling's life has always captivated public interest. 

    Now, as she seeks a new beginning, the accomplished actress has made a lavish decision for her and her family.

    Tori, best known for her iconic role in the '90s drama series, has transitioned from her recent stays in RVs and motels with her five children to a luxurious rental home. 

    Situated in the serene Woodland Hills neighborhood of Los Angeles, this opulent residence comes with a monthly price tag of $18,000, as reported by the Daily Mail.

    Encompassing 3,300 square feet, this single-story property nestled in the heart of San Fernando Valley is nothing short of exquisite. 

    It boasts four spacious bedrooms and three meticulously designed bathrooms. An additional guest house with its own bathroom is the cherry on top.

    Reflecting a blend of comfort and luxury, the interiors are "exquisitely furnished" with an abundance of natural light streaming in. 

    The residence proudly showcases hardwood floors, expansive sliding glass doors, contemporary flat screen TVs, and a dedicated gaming room. The plush furniture adds an inviting touch, creating a space that feels both upscale and homey.

    But the allure doesn't end indoors. The house opens up to a grand pool area, perfect for Los Angeles' warm weather. 

    Adjacent to it is a patio that doubles as an outdoor entertainment zone, accompanied by a dining area tailored to accommodate up to six guests, making it ideal for intimate gatherings.

    Recently, Tori, 50, was spotted scouting moving trucks and storage facilities around Los Angeles, hinting at her transition.

    This year has been a whirlwind of emotions for Tori. In June, her husband of nearly two decades, Dean McDermott, took to Instagram to announce their separation. 

    The heart-wrenching post, which has since been removed, read, “It’s with great sadness and a very very heavy heart that after 18 years together and 5 amazing children, that @torispelling and I have decided to go our separate ways, and start a new journey of our own.”

    Post-separation, Tori and her children—Liam, 16, Stella, 15, Hattie, 12, Finn, 11, and Beau, 6—experienced a period of instability, shifting between various rentals, motels, and an RV. 

    To add to the complications, they had to vacate one of their rentals due to a security threat involving their neighbor.

    In another twist, Dean, previously the host of Chopped Canada was seen at the Department of Social Services in Los Angeles in mid-October. 

    Witnesses mentioned him being in high spirits, laughing, and even sharing jokes. He was accompanied by Lily Calo, rumored to be his new romantic interest.
