    Cozumel, a popular tourist destination in Mexico, is generally considered safe for tourists. The island has a relatively low crime rate compared to other parts of Mexico, and violent crime against tourists is rare. According to the Crime Index 2023 Report by Numbeo, Cozumel has a crime index of 34.38, which is considered low on their scale. The U.S. State Department has not imposed any specific restrictions on visitors to Cozumel other than advising to exercise increased caution.

    Is Cozumel safer than Cancun?

    While both Cancun and Cozumel are generally safe destinations, as a solo female traveler, I feel safer in Cozumel. Cozumel has a lower rate of violent crime and petty crimes compared to Cancun.

    Is it safe to walk at night in Cozumel?

    Yes, it is safe to walk in Cozumel during both day and night. The safety rating index for the island remains high, above 70. From personal experience, walking in Cozumel, even at night, feels safe and secure.

    Is Cozumel safe from drug cartel?

    Cozumel is generally considered safe from drug cartel activities. Concerns about drug cartel activities may exist, but Cozumel has a relatively low crime rate and is popular among tourists. These concerns are often based on misconceptions rather than actual threats.

    Do cartels operate in Cozumel?

    Cozumel, being a popular tourist destination, may have some potential hazards caused by drug cartels in the area. Tourists should always be cautious, but overall, the presence of cartels in Cozumel is not a major concern.

    What should I be careful of in Cozumel?

    When in Cozumel, it is advisable not to flash jewelry or large amounts of cash in public. While Cozumel is generally a safe place, it’s always wise to take precautions to avoid being an easy target for theft or scams.

    Is it safe to take a taxi in Cozumel?

    Cabs in Cozumel are relatively safe, and the drivers are known to be friendly. Many of them speak English, which can ease communication for tourists. It is recommended to use registered taxis and agree on the fare before getting in the taxi.

    Can you drink water in Cozumel?

    It is not recommended to drink tap water in Cozumel. Although the water supply is chlorinated to some extent, it is still advisable to avoid drinking tap water anywhere on the island. It is best to use bottled water for drinking and brushing teeth.

    Can I use US money in Cozumel?

    Most tourist establishments in Cozumel accept both pesos and US dollars. However, it is common for change to be given in pesos, and not all places may have a large supply of cash to make change for large bills.

    What is the best time of year to go to Cozumel?

    The best time to visit Cozumel is from March to June when the island enjoys warm temperatures with daytime highs around 90 degrees Fahrenheit and nighttime temperatures in the mid-70s. Winters can be slightly cooler, with temperatures ranging between the upper 60s and lower 80s.

    What is the safest tourist destination in Mexico?

    Are mosquitoes bad in Cozumel?

    There is a high risk of mosquito activity in Cozumel. It is advisable to use EPA-registered mosquito repellent to protect yourself from mosquito bites while visiting the island.

    Is it safe to drink ice in Cozumel?

    Although the tap water in Cozumel is chlorinated to some extent, it is not recommended to drink ice made from tap water. All ice served in restaurants and hotels on the island is purified, so there should be no concerns about consuming ice served in these establishments.

    Do you need a US passport to go to Cozumel Mexico?

    A valid passport book is required to enter Mexico by air. If you only have a U.S. passport card, you may be denied admission when entering at an airport. It is advisable to have a valid passport book when traveling to Cozumel.

    Can you just walk around Cozumel?

    Cozumel is a pedestrian-friendly destination, especially in the downtown area. It is generally safe to walk around Cozumel, and there is a relaxed atmosphere throughout the island. However, it is always important to be aware of your surroundings and take necessary precautions.
