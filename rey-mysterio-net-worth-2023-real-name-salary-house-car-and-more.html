    Rey is a former World heavyweight champion, International champion, and Tag team champion along with his other achievements in the WWE. Rey is the first-ever superstar who won a tag team championship with his son Dominik Mysterio as a father-son duo. At present, Rey Mysterio was inducted as a Hall of Famer in the WWE's Hall of Fame class of 2023.
