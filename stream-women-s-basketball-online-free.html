    The Iowa vs. South Carolina women’s basketball game takes place Sunday, April 7 at 12 p.m. PT/3 p.m. ET live from Rocket Mortgage FieldHouse in Cleveland, Ohio. You can watch the Hawkeyes vs. Gamecocks on TV through ABC.
