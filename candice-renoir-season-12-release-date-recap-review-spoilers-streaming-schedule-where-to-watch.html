    The Plot of Candice Renoir

    At the center of the series is Candice Renoir, a detective who temporarily steps away from her career to focus on raising her children. Following a challenging divorce, Candice reenters the workforce, confronting the dual hurdles of apprehending criminals and adapting to shifts within the department. Her comeback triggers tensions with colleagues who perceive her promotion as overshadowing their contributions. However, despite the initial challenges, Candice ultimately demonstrates her prowess as a skilled investigator.
