      Summer is almost here, and with heatwaves likely on the way, garden sun loungers have never been higher on our wishlists.

      Our trusty sofas are about to take a back seat and we plan to spend the rest of our downtime relaxing on the comfiest and most stylish sun lounger we can get our hands on. As one of the most sought after pieces of garden furniture, they're already selling fast, so make sure to get your order in quickly.

      From John Lewis to Argos, and Cox & Cox to OKA, we've searched for the best sun loungers to transform your garden this summer...

      • M&S Collection Marlow Sun Lounger

        Marks & Spencer's Marlowe lounger has a gently curving, cushion-topped seat and a natural-look, hand-woven rattan base. Order it now and take 25% off in the garden furniture sale.

        £299.25 at M&S

      • Lafuma Air Comfort Futura Sun Lounger

        This reclining sun lounger chair from John Lewis is a bestseller thanks to its comfort and price point. Foldable and hardwearing, it also features back support and resistance to UV exposure.

        £299.99 at John Lewis

      • Cox & Cox Beanbag Lounger

        Truly bringing the indoors out, this Cox & Cox beanbag lounger has been specially designed with durable splash-proof fabric that will also keep you cool in the heat.

        £375 at Cox & Cox

      • SONGMICS Sun Lounger

        This popular SONGMICS reclining sun lounger features an overhead shade - perfect for reading or sleeping without any disturbance from the sun.

        £64.99 at Amazon

      • B&Q Curacao Cabana Sun Lounger

        Looking for a bargain? You can shop this striped B&Q sun lounger for just £32. Lightweight, foldable and easily portable, it also has three reclining positions.

        £32 at B&Q

      • Dunelm Sulu Sun Lounger

        Designed with rust-proof aluminium and weather-resistant polywood, Dunelm's rattan lounger is as durable as it is beautifully designed. It features generously filled seat cushions for ultimate comfort.

        £299 at Dunelm

      • Outsunny Texteline Sun Lounger

        Built on wheels, Outsunny's classic sun lounger can be moved around easily, which is ideal when you're trying to catch the sun. It's made from acacia wood that's both highly durable and weather resistant.

        £108.99 at Aosom

      • OKA Pentire Adjustable Sun Lounger

        With its woven base and cream cushions, this rattan sun lounger is beautifully bohemian. It's also practical, complete with an adjustable back that collapses completely flat when needed.

        £840 at OKA

      • Argos Home Peacan Sun Lounger

        We think this sleek sun lounger from Argos looks more expensive than its price tag. It has a rust-resistant frame and reviews say it's super comfortable.

        £95 at Argos
