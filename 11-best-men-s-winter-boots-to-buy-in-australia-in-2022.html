    In the depths of winter, there’s only one type of footwear guaranteed to get you through your day in equal parts style, comfort and warmth: a trusty pair of boots.

    You may be more of a streetstyle sneaker guy or even just a laid-back thongs devotee in the summertime, but when the weather cools down, a pair of boots is a wardrobe essential – and I’m here to tell you there’s a pair to suit every taste.

    RELATED: Our picks for best women’s boots to wear through winter

    Want something to take you from the office to a night out? A pair of Chelsea boots will have your back. Looking for a more effortless, rustic look? You can’t pass up on a lace-up pair of Docs or Timberlands. In need of something durable and hard-wearing? A stylish pair of work boots are a winner.

    Trust me – whatever your style, there’s a boot for you.

    WHAT WINTER BOOTS ARE IN STYLE?

    A pair of Chelsea boots – instantly recognisable for their exposed elastic gussets and pull tabs – are always in style year after year, and well suited to work or leisure. Meanwhile, a pair of lace-up Timberlands or Doc Martens are perfect for casual, everyday styling.
