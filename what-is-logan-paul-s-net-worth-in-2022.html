    Logan Paul has just joined the WWE, but what is his net worth?

    Published Jul 2, 2022, 17:39:27 GMT+1Last updated Jul 2, 2022, 17:39:25 GMT+1

    Logan Paul, 27, is an American YouTuber and podcast host who found fame after posting a series of popular Vine videos.

    The social media star has recently dabbled back into combat sports, following a successful junior wrestling career.

    In 2018 he fought British YouTuber KSI at the Manchester Arena, and last year he fought five-division world champion Floyd 'Money' Mayweather in an exhibition bout.

    Now Paul is set to take the WWE world by storm, following the announcement that he has signed a deal with the company.

    He is set to face off with The Miz at Summerslam at the end of the month.

    Love him or hate him, it's hard not to be familiar with Logan Paul and his antics, but what is he worth?

    What Is Logan Paul’s Net Worth?

    As of 2022, the American is worth an estimated $45 million (£37.3 million) according to celebritynetworths.

    This wealth is the sum-total of advertising revenue from his youtube videos, his lucrative line of merchandising and money earned from boxing and wrestling.

    Paul has also appeared as a more conventional actor in addition to his Youtube content, notably on law and order, and Stitchers. 

    Despite his evident talent as an entertainer, Paul has never been far from controversy. 

    In 2018 the American posted a video showing the body of an apparent suicide victim in a forest in Japan.

    Paul filmed his reaction to encountering the corpse of a recently deceased man. 

    The video triggered a wave of outrage from social media users and politicians, and it almost ended his career.
