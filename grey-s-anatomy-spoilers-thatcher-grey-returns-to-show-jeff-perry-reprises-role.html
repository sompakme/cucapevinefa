    Greys Anatomy spoilers: Thatcher Grey returns to show, Jeff Perry reprises role

    Greys Anatomy spoilers indicate that Thatcher Grey (Jeff Perry) is returning to the show. This is big news, as the character is the father of Meredith Grey, who has not always had the best relationship with his daughter.

    08 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Grey’s Anatomy spoilers indicate that Thatcher Grey (Jeff Perry) is returning to the show. This is big news, as the character is the father of Meredith Grey, who has not always had the best relationship with his daughter.

    A preview of the upcoming episode was revealed showing Webber speaking with Meredith. He mentions that her father is sick and it fades to black. It’s some foreshadowing about the upcoming episode, which should be an emotional one for the main character, as well as the viewers.

    A video of that preview for Grey’s Anatomy Season 15 Episode 6 titled Flowers Grow Out Of My Grave can be seen below.

    Some sunshine for your Sunday #GreysAnatomy pic.twitter.com/tOi85fUAPb

    Jeff Perry as Thatcher Grey

    The actor who plays Thatcher Grey has been around in films and on television for a number of years. He hasn’t appeared on Grey’s Anatomy since 2011, though, missing the death of his other daughter on the show. That could lead to some negative feelings from Meredith, even as she deals with her father being sick.

    It’s all happening! The last 5 minutes will have you feeling all the feels! ? #GreysAnatomy pic.twitter.com/YGMulN64iV

    — Greys Anatomy (@GreysABC) October 30, 2018

    In addition to his time as Thatcher Grey, Jeff Perry spent a lot of time as Cyrus Beene on ABC’s Scandal. This was a primary reason why the character of Thatcher hasn’t been seen for a number of years.

    His last appearance on the show happened right before the series premiere of Scandal aired on ABC. Before his time with Shonda Rhimes, Perry was Inspector Harvey Leek on Nash Bridges for a number of years.

    Grey’s Anatomy Season 16 spoilers

    The episode where Meredith’s father reappears is called Flowers Grow Out of My Grave. There are several storylines, including Teddy trying to tell Owen that she is pregnant, a patient whose family is celebrating Day of the Dead, and remembrance of loved ones lost by current cast members.

    That will lead up to where Richard Webber speaks to Meredith about her father and we can find out how she reacts to Thatcher Grey’s return.

    Grey’s Anatomy airs Thursday nights at 8/7c on ABC.
