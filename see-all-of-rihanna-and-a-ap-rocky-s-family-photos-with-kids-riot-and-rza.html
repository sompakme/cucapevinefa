    Rihanna and A$AP Rocky are settling into life as parents of two—and they just shared a first-time, rare glimpse of their family.

    The couple—who welcomed their second child, baby boy Riot Rose, in August—posed in adorable family portraits captured by celebrity photographer Miles Diggs. The photos included their first child, son RZA Athelston, too.

    In the sweet pictures, the family showed off their coordinating style. Rihanna looked glamorous in navy blue leggings that extended into stiletto boots, paired with a baggy denim jacket and layers of gold chain necklaces. Rocky wore three major pieces by Bottega Veneta: a ribbed white tank top, denim printed leather pants, and ankle boots. He finished off the look with a green flannel button-up and a diamond-encrusted chain necklace.

    Meanwhile, little RZA seemed to take after his fashionable parents in a tiny black leather motorcycle jacket, a gray and red T-shirt, faded blue jeans, and Fenty x Puma's Avanti sneakers. As for newborn Riot, Rihanna swaddled the infant in a fuzzy blue, pink, and white blanket. Underneath, Riot looked adorable in a baby pink onesie and matching beanie, plus tan lace-up boots.
