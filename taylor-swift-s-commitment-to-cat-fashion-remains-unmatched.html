    People tend to talk about Taylor Swift's style choices in terms of eras: her high-fashion, gothic-like looks from "Reputation;" her super feminine, fit-and-flares that came with the "Red" album; the colorful, rainbow ensembles we now associate with "Lover." A small description of where the singer was, musically and romantically, and we can instantly picture what she was wearing. Through it all, however, Swift's commitment to cat fashion has remained consistent.

    As Swifties well know, the singer is a proud cat lady. She has three fur babies of her own — Olivia Benson, Meredith Grey, and Benjamin Button — who she often speaks about in interviews or posts about on social media. Heck, Swift even had a role in the movie Cats! So, it's really no surprise that, throughout her career, she's also enjoyed cat fashion, rocking images of the animal on bags, shoes, shirts, and more. Even as her aesthetic shifts and she adopts a sparklier, '70s-inspired vibe for her "Midnights" era, Swift's love of cat fashion continues.

    So, it's time to give our girl (and her longtime stylist Joseph Cassell) some credit, applauding her passion for cat pieces and highlighting a few of our favorites.

    October 2022

    We're well into the "Midnights" era now, but Swift hasn't abandoned her cute cat gear. While announcing her tracklist on TikTok, the singer wore a Linda Lori Vintage T-shirt with a white cat graphic. Earlier in the year, she was also spotted in a Stella McCartney cat sweater.

    January 2020

    One viral moment from Swift's Miss Americana documentary was this shot of her cat carrier, which was just as fun and fashionable as it was practical for traveling with pets.

    April 2019

    Swift might have missed the dress code memo for Gigi Hadid's denim-themed birthday party, but she did show up wearing this cat purse, so all is forgiven on our end.

    August 2018

    Sometimes the perfect summer look includes denim shorts and a T-shirt. And if that T-shirt includes a cat print? Even better.

    August 2018

    Model material! We're talking about Olivia, obviously, whose face is featured on Swift's earrings.

    July 2018

    Did we just time travel? This graphic cat tee is very kindergartener-in-the-'90s, and, to be honest, we're not mad about it.

    July 2018

    That's not just any drawing of a cat wearing glasses — it's a drawing of Swift's cat, Meredith, wearing glasses.

    August 2017

    The old Taylor was dead and gone in the "Look What You Made Me Do" music video. The new one upgraded from a kitten to a Gucci tiger.

    November 2016

    Swift wasn't just representing her favorite animal with this statement-making spotted coat, she was also wearing a top with a kitten on it (and the kitten was wearing a crown).

    August 2015

    Before you let those cat heels distract you, check out Swift's cat purse. Double cats!

    March 2015

    We have a feeling that if we raided the singer's closet, we'd find a number of cat bags like this one.

    August 2014

    The "Shake It Off" video was full of amazing outfits, but this cat sweatshirt definitely caught our eye.

    March 2013

    Ear headbands were all the rage in the early 2010s and Swift was clearly on board with the whimsical trend.

    November 2012

    One of these polka dots is, in fact, the face of a little kitty.

    October 2012

    Cats can be fancy, too. Swfit spiced up her formal fit-and-flare with cat heels when attending the BBC Radio 1 Teen Awards.

    October 2012

    Retro cat! Swift paired her feminine, floral midi skirt with white cat flats.

    January 2012

    It takes a moment to notice, but that's a cat print on Swift's dress.
