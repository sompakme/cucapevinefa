    Since her breakup with Leonardo DiCaprio, fans have been eager to find out Camila Morrone's thoughts regarding the whole ordeal. According to a source close to the actor, the "Death Wish" star has been focusing on work and her friendships. "Camila is doing fine. She has a big group of friends that really care about her," they revealed to People. As for Morrone and DiCaprio potentially making up, the source said reconciliation is unlikely. "She is moving on with her life. She is not in contact with Leo," the source added.

    While Morrone has shifted her focus to friends and work, DiCaprio has allegedly planned his return to the dating field. According to a report from Us Weekly, the "Inception" star has reportedly been making moves to woo supermodel Gigi Hadid. "Leo does have his sights on Gigi, but she hasn't shown any interest," a source revealed. "They're friends, but she doesn't want to be romantic with [him] right now." Even though DiCaprio and Morrone have called it quits, it seems like the two are going to be just fine.
