    Kobe Bryant‘s legacy is being remembered in a special edition of NBA2K24, the best-selling basketball game for Nintendo, PlayStation and Xbox.

    For Black Friday, the NBA 2K24 Kobe Bryant Edition for PS5 and Xbox Series X is on sale, apiece, for $30, or half off, at Amazon, as the latest release in the annual video game franchise. The athletes chosen to appear on the cover of the game are always a hotly debated topic (last year’s NBA 2K23 cover featured Devin Booker of the Phoenix Suns) but this year’s cover art is a no-brainer, with No. 24 fronting the 2024 edition.

    Related Stories

    In addition, the Nintendo Switch, PS4 and Xbox One versions of the game are also on sale for $30, or half off, at Amazon.

    The fan-favorite series honors the hall of fame basketball player with spectacular cover art, enhanced graphics and responsive gameplay across consoles. 

    NBA 2K24 Kobe Bryant Edition pays tribute to the “Black Mamba” with his signature skills, legendary performances and iconic swagger.

    In the game, you play as Bryant during his early days as an up-and-coming basketball phenom to become one of the NBA’s all-time greatest players. The game also features up-to-date team rosters, next-level gameplay with realistic visuals, a card-collecting mode with hundreds of customizable options and much more. Meanwhile, “ProPLAY” is a new immersive feature for NBA 2K24. It converts real NBA film footage into actual gameplay for a more realistic experience.

    “NBA 2K24 is a special entry for us because we get to celebrate one of basketball’s greatest players of all time — Kobe Bryant,” says Greg Thomas, president at Visual Concepts, in a press release.

    “We’ve ushered in a new era of 2K, thanks to the groundbreaking ProPLAY technology delivering the most authentic and realistic NBA 2K gameplay to date,” continues Thomas. “NBA 2K24 also introduces new updates to our beloved modes with incredible details that will delight gamers and basketball fans alike.”

    You can pick up a copy of NBA 2K24 Kobe Bryant Edition for yourself at Amazon and Target.

    Bryant was a five-time NBA champ and 18-time all-star. He tragically died with his 13-year-old daughter, Gianna, in a helicopter crash in January 2020.
