    Hailey Bieber Went Full Matrix With No Pants and a Sweeping Leather Trench Coat

    25 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Just as we predicted, Hailey Bieber’s tour de force around Paris — which has included the unexpected fall shoe combo we did not anticipate — is off to a fabulous start. On Monday, the beauty mogul left her pants in the States while simultaneously giving us a luxe take on edgy Matrix energy.

    While heading to dinner at Hôtel Costes restaurant, the Rhode founder slipped into not just any pair of shorts, but black high-waisted hot pants by Petar Petrov. She teamed the hot pants with a tan turtleneck layered under a floor-sweeping black trench coat. Hailey covered her legs with Calzedonia sheer black tights and accessorized with a pair of black velvet pumps. Slim black sunglasses, silver statement earrings, a black Jil Sander handbag, and a coordinating belt with a gold metallic buckle at the waist provided the finishing touches to her evening look.

    Her brunette bob was pulled back for a slicked, tight bun with a major middle part, while the rest of her glam included a dewy complexion, dark pink glossy lips, and rosy cheeks.

    Earlier that day, Hailey's wardrobe started off on a lighter note that solidified our obsession with teeny tiny ballet flats once and for all. Detailing the OOTD on her Instagram story, Hailey wore a bubblegum pink minidress that featured an off-the-shoulder top and a flared, bouncy skirt. Ushering in the ultimate shoe combo of fall, she teamed her ultra-short dress with a pair of black Mary Janes and white crew socks. The cutesy details continued via her half-up, half-down hairstyle complete with a black velvet bow barrette by Jennifer Behr.
